Why REVOGUE Exists

Solving "I have nothing to wear" — with clothes you already own.

REVOGUE was built to prove that better styling doesn't require a bigger wardrobe — just better ideas.

🎯

The Problem

Most people wear a small fraction of their wardrobe on repeat, simply because they can't picture new ways to style pieces they already own — leading to unnecessary shopping and waste.

💡

Our Approach

REVOGUE lets you upload your own clothes and get instant, personalised styling ideas for any occasion — factoring in your age and location — plus accessory pairings to complete the look.

Project Journey

How REVOGUE was built

1. Research

We studied how people currently decide what to wear, and how styling apps and AI assistants approach outfit recommendations.

2. Design

We designed a simple, aesthetic experience: upload your closet, share your context (occasion, age, location), and receive a styled look.

3. Build

REVOGUE is built as a fully client-side web app — HTML, CSS and JavaScript — so it can be hosted freely and opened from anywhere via GitHub Pages.

4. Style Intelligence

The AI Style Advisor combines occasion, age group and location into styling suggestions, with accessory ideas linked to Amazon.

Academic Transparency

About the prototype

This is a school project. To keep REVOGUE fully self-hosted on GitHub Pages (a static file host with no server or database), a few things are simulated for demonstration purposes:
  • Style suggestions are generated by a rule-based template engine in the browser — designed to mirror how a real Google Gemini API integration would behave in production.
  • Photos shown across the site (hero images, team photos, generated "styled look" photos) are placeholder images, not real product or personal photography.
  • Login accounts and uploaded closet photos are stored only in your browser's local storage — there is no real server or database, and no cloud backup.
  • Accessory suggestions link to live Amazon search results rather than specific curated products.
